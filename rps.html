Instructions To Play

Objective:

To defeat your opponent by choosing the correct hand symbol.

Choose your hand symbol:

  • Rock: A closed fist.Or click r to choose Rock.
  • Paper: An open hand, flat and facing downwards.Or click p to choose Paper.
  • Scissors: A fist with index and middle fingers extended to form a V shape.Or click s to choose Scissors.

Rules:

  • Rock crushes Scissors.
  • Scissors cuts Paper.
  • Paper covers Rock.
  • If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie.
